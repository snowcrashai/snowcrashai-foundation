# AI Pentesting Playbooks

SnowcrashAI Pentesting Playbooks provide **hands-on, security-focused guidance** for testing AI systems under real-world adversarial conditions.

Each playbook is designed to be:
- Reproducible
- Defensive-aware
- Platform-agnostic
- Suitable for red team and blue team use

Playbooks describe **how attacks work**, **what signals to monitor**, and **how to mitigate risk**.
